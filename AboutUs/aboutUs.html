About Us

The Foxfit Way

What We Do

At FoxFit we specialize in seriously fun and effective core fitness training.At the center of our program is the concept that before you attempt to master the heavy lifting or any machines,you must first learn how to master your body.

We combine the old school with the new tech.We offer programs in which you learn how to eat healthy,live a balanced life and along with that we offer coaching one on one to help you become a better version of yourself.

Our method

How We Do It

Everyone talks about the importance of strengthening your core.We all know that core training can improve strength,flexibiliy,and posture while also rehabilitating your lower back and correcting other related health issues.

But what almost no one understands is the concept of back-to-basics high intensity core training and how it incorporates the smaller muscles of your core with the larger muscles,and uses your very own body weight to not only get you in the best shape of your life but also to protect you from injuries.

Our Mission

Why We Do It

We believe that acces to health and fitness it is a human right. Everyday we strive to create a workout environment that is inviting,accepting and motivating for the people of all walks of life.Everyday we serve to educate and empower our fitness community and the city of Dublin by creating long lasting healthy habits that leads to better wellness and a respect and appreciation for all bodies-no matter the size,shape,color or background.
